1 - How it works

By downloading the datatreats suite of apps to your smartphone and wearable devices the world will become personalized to you. We have partnerships with a variety of different organizations that will provide you with different “treats” that are specifically tailored to you.

2 - Why it works

Sensors! Sensors are now being packed into every corner of every device and they are creating a plethora of information on you. Right now, however, this information has been relatively fragmented and as a result not living up to it’s full potential. datatreats is changing that by allowing you to choose which information you share with which companies and in turn, they offer you a personalized “treat”.

3 - Privacy

Datatreats philosophy on privacy is that you should own all of your personal data and thus have the ability to choose who you would like to share your data with. datatreats is simply the platform where we allow you to choose who gets access to your data. But, if you find that they used your data irresponsibly, we give you the power to rate the company on how effectively and responsibly they used your data.
